Abstract

Throughout any programmer's professional career, there is a struggle to showcase in-depth projects and experiences while also mainting a sense of brevity for the sake of a recruiter's time and sanity. While other works have also delved into this area, see Resume and LinkedIn, in this work we aim to explore the professional life and accomplishments of Trevor Ortega at a level of depth and pragmatism that is previously unfounded.
